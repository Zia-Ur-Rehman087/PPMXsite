Who we are

PPMx is an innovative design studio
run by a creative team of architects,
urban planners, landscape and interior architects,
and graphic designers.

Our team undertake a vast scope of challenging projects and offer thoughtful, sustainable solutions as their response committed to inspiring architecture, urban design, landscape architecture, and wayfinding services.

Our team has demonstrated that progressive ideas and technology can be combined with the traditional and epoch practice of architecture and urban design and landscape architecture to create solutions that minimize impacts on the environment and provide sustainable urbanism for future generations. We adopt the idea of a better world through synergies between nature, technology, spatial form, and placemaking. ​

Our philosophy it explicitly represents a creative culture focusing on the three “P’: People Life, Place Meaning, and Place Productivity, where a desire for fulfilling our distinctive lifestyles honors lasting relationships between people and the place of their life movement.

Our Approach

High design skills with a professional accountability that reflects the contribution towards advancing the community's lifestyle and quality of life by promoting cultural values, sustainable design principles, local climate, natural lighting, geometrical structure, innovation in building system, and local materials.

We work in every endeavor of architectural and urban design innovation to create and produce distinctive and expressive environments through manufacturing design quality and unique features of places that ensure the balance with technology, art and social lifestyle. As well as, we put out energy in every endeavor of urban design principles, processes, and benchmarks that helps delight the imagination of users and visitors.

We pay close attention to client needs, requirements and desires, as well as completing professional commitments within established time and budgetary limits and meticulous technical accomplishment are the PPMx's well known characteristics.
